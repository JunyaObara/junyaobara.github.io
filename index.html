GenAI Privacy Risk Triage PoC

Turn early-stage software specs into actionable privacy risk triage in minutes
— with evidence-ready outputs and Human-in-the-Loop (HITL) routing to reduce legal review bottlenecks.

CIPA VPPA BIPA Audit-style evidence packaging Conservative HITL escalation Designed for repeatable adoption
Solution Brief (PDF) 90-sec Demo LinkedIn

What it does

  • Issue spotting: flags privacy risk items per law (CIPA/VPPA/BIPA).
  • Routing: BLOCKER / NEEDS_REVIEW / CLEARED with conservative thresholds.
  • Evidence packaging: structured audit-style JSON with references to relevant legal text chunks.
  • Remediation: suggests requirement changes (final decision stays human/legal).
GTM angle: This is built as a repeatable motion—fast triage, clear outputs, and predictable review workload—so teams can scale adoption with consistent quality.

Why it matters

Privacy risks found late can trigger rework, delays, and costly legal escalations. This PoC targets earlier detection at the spec stage and makes downstream review more efficient.
