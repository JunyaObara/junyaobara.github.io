GenAI Privacy Risk Triage PoC

Early-stage spec → legal risk triage in minutes, with evidence-ready outputs and Human-in-the-Loop (HITL) routing.

CIPA VPPA BIPA Audit-style evidence Conservative routing (HITL) Designed for scale

Problem (Why it matters)

Privacy risks are often discovered late (after requirements freeze), causing expensive rework and legal review bottlenecks. Teams need a fast, repeatable way to surface high-risk items early and package evidence for review.

What this PoC delivers

  • Risk triage: flags high-risk items and summarizes issues per law.
  • Routing: BLOCKER / NEEDS_REVIEW / CLEARED with conservative HITL escalation.
  • Evidence-ready output: audit-log style structured JSON and citations to relevant legal text chunks.
  • Recommendations: suggests requirement changes to reduce risk (final decision remains human).

How it works (repeatable motion)

  • Input: software requirement/spec text (SRS/PRD fragments).
  • Processing: identify risk patterns per law, request missing evidence, and apply conservative thresholds.
  • Output: structured findings + evidence package + recommended requirement changes.
  • Operational design: ambiguous items are routed to HITL to minimize false negatives on high-risk items.
GTM framing: the goal is not “perfect accuracy,” but an evaluation framework that balances regulatory risk (avoid missing high-risk items) and review workload (reduce unnecessary human review).
